Biangular Lat Row

Main Muscle: Lats

  1. Adjust the seat to your appropriate height (Top of the chest support pad located at mid to upper chest).
  2. Chest support pad to your appropriate arm length (Handles should be little beyond arm’s length).
  3. Select training weight by using both weight stack pins.
  4. Reach forward grip triple pivot handles.
  5. Rotate the handles to your desired stating position.
  6. Tighten thighs against pads.
  7. Pull the handles till your elbows reach 90 degrees.
  8. Then let arms go back to slightly bent arms.
